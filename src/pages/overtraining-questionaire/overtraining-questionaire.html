Overtraining questionaire

Overtraining is the result of giving your body more work or stress than it can handle. Overtraining occurs when a person experiences stress and physical trauma from exercise faster than their body can repair the damage. Although, regular exercise is extremely beneficial to your general health and fitness, exercise is a stress for the body and breaks it down. It’s the rest and recovery that makes you stronger and healthier.
