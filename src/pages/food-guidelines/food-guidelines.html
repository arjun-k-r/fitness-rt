Food guidelines Vata Pitta Kapha

General tips

  • choose to eat warm, moist, cooked, and generally well spiced foods, instead of cold, frozen, dry, and raw;
  • focus on the sweet, sour, and salty tastes and limit the astrigent, bitter, and pungent tastes;
  • eat regular meals;
  • avoid stimulants.

Note: The following foods are just guidelines and should be adjusted to each one's individuality (food allergies, seasons, degree of dosha predominance or aggravation, etc.).
